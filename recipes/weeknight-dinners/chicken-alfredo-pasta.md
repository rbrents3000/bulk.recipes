# Chicken Alfredo Pasta

> A weeknight cheat code — Costco rotisserie chicken and Kirkland alfredo sauce turn penne and broccoli into a creamy, satisfying dinner with almost no real cooking.

**Prep:** 10 min | **Cook:** 10 min | **Servings:** 6 | **Cost:** ~$2.75/serving

## Costco Shopping List
- Kirkland Signature Rotisserie Chicken — 1 whole, shredded (~$4.99, 3 lb chicken)
- Kirkland Signature Alfredo Sauce — 1 jar (~$7.99, 3-pack/24 oz jars)
- Kirkland Signature Penne Pasta — 1 lb (~$8.49, 4 lb box)
- Kirkland Signature Broccoli Florets (frozen) — 3 cups (~$7.49, 4 lb bag)

## Other Ingredients
- 1/2 cup Pasta water (reserved from cooking)
- 1/2 teaspoon Garlic powder
- 1/4 teaspoon Black pepper
- 1/4 cup Grated Parmesan *(optional)*
- Pinch Red pepper flakes *(optional)*

## Instructions
1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook penne according to package directions, about 10 minutes. Add frozen broccoli florets to the pot during the last 3 minutes of cooking. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water, then drain.
2. While pasta cooks, shred the rotisserie chicken into bite-sized pieces. You'll need about 3 cups of shredded meat — save the rest for another meal.
3. Return the pot to medium heat. Pour in the alfredo sauce and stir in garlic powder and pepper. Warm until just simmering.
4. Add the drained pasta, broccoli, and shredded chicken. Toss everything together, adding splashes of pasta water until the sauce coats the pasta nicely.
5. Serve in bowls topped with Parmesan and red pepper flakes if you like.

> **Tip:** Shred the rotisserie chicken while it's still warm — it pulls apart much more easily. If you bought it the day before, microwave it for a minute to loosen it up.

## Storage
Refrigerate for up to 4 days. The sauce thickens as it sits — add a splash of milk or broth when reheating on the stove or in the microwave.

## Leftover Ideas
- Spread into a baking dish, top with mozzarella, and broil for a quick chicken alfredo bake
- Stuff into flour tortillas and pan-fry for creamy chicken alfredo quesadillas
