# Greek Chicken Meal Prep

> Mediterranean-style meal prep with juicy marinated chicken thighs, fluffy rice, crisp veggies, tangy feta, and a drizzle of tzatziki.

**Prep:** 20 min | **Cook:** 25 min | **Servings:** 6 | **Cost:** ~$3.00/serving

## Costco Shopping List
- Kirkland Boneless Skinless Chicken Thighs — 2.5 lbs (~$7.50, from 6.5 lb pack at ~$2.99/lb)
- Kirkland Jasmine Rice — 3 cups cooked (~$0.60, 25 lb bag)
- Kirkland Feta Cheese Crumbles — 3/4 cup (~$2.00, 24 oz container)
- Kirkland Organic Cucumber — 2 medium (~$1.50, 3-pack)
- Kirkland Grape Tomatoes — 1.5 cups halved (~$2.00, 2 lb clamshell)
- Kirkland Tzatziki Dip — 6 tbsp (~$1.80, 35 oz tub)

## Other Ingredients
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp dried thyme
- 1 lemon, juiced and zested
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
- Kalamata olives (optional)
- Pita bread for serving (optional)

## Instructions
1. Make the marinade by whisking together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, minced garlic, oregano, thyme, lemon juice, lemon zest, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
2. Trim any excess fat from the chicken thighs and place them in a large zip-top bag or container. Pour the marinade over the chicken and make sure every piece is coated. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or up to overnight for maximum flavor.
3. Cook the jasmine rice according to package directions. Fluff with a fork and let it cool slightly.
4. While the rice cooks, preheat a large skillet or grill pan over medium-high heat. Remove the chicken from the marinade and shake off any excess.
5. Cook the chicken thighs for 5-7 minutes per side until they develop a nice golden-brown sear and the internal temperature reaches 165°F. Let them rest for 5 minutes, then slice against the grain into strips.
6. While everything cooks, prep your veggies: dice the cucumbers, halve the grape tomatoes, and thinly slice the red onion.
7. Divide the rice among 6 meal prep containers. Arrange the sliced chicken alongside the rice. Add the diced cucumber, tomatoes, red onion, and a generous sprinkle of feta crumbles to each container.
8. Pack the tzatziki in small separate containers or sauce cups -- about 1 tablespoon per serving. Add olives if using.

> **Tip:** Marinate the chicken the night before and the morning assembly goes much faster. If you want to cook everything even quicker, cut the thighs into cubes before marinating and cook them in a single layer -- they'll be done in about 8 minutes total.

## Storage
Keeps in the fridge for up to 5 days. Store tzatziki separately to keep everything fresh. Reheat the chicken and rice in the microwave for 2-3 minutes, but keep the veggies and feta on the side so they stay crisp and cool. The chicken also works well eaten cold straight from the fridge.

## Leftover Ideas
- Stuff everything into a warm pita for a quick Greek chicken wrap
- Toss the chicken and veggies over mixed greens for a Mediterranean salad
- Chop the chicken and fold into a Greek-style pasta salad with orzo and lemon vinaigrette
