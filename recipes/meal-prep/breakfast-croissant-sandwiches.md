# Breakfast Croissant Sandwiches

> Use Costco's bakery croissants to make loaded breakfast sandwiches. Egg, bacon, cheese — meal prep a batch and freeze them for the week.

**Prep:** 10 min | **Cook:** 15 min | **Servings:** 6 | **Cost:** ~$2.33/serving

## Costco Shopping List
- Kirkland Signature Butter Croissants — 6 croissants (~$5.99, 12 count)
- Kirkland Signature Thick-Sliced Bacon — 12 slices (~$18.99, 4 x 1 lb packs)
- Kirkland Signature Large Eggs — 6 eggs (~$7.99, 5 dozen)
- Kirkland Signature Shredded Mexican Blend Cheese — 1 cup (~$12.99, 2.5 lb bag)

## Other Ingredients
- to taste Salt and pepper
- 1 sliced Avocado *(optional)*
- to taste Hot sauce *(optional)*

## Instructions
1. Cook bacon in a skillet until crispy. Set aside on paper towels.
2. In the same pan (drain most of the grease), cook eggs to your preference — fried, scrambled, or as thin omelets that fit the croissant.
3. Slice croissants in half horizontally.
4. Layer: bottom croissant half, cheese, egg, 2 slices bacon, avocado if using, top half.
5. Eat immediately, or wrap in foil and bake at 350°F for 5 minutes to melt the cheese and warm through.

> **Tip:** For meal prep: wrap assembled sandwiches (without avocado) individually in parchment then foil. Freeze. Reheat from frozen in a 350°F oven for 20 minutes or microwave 90 seconds.

## Storage
Refrigerate assembled sandwiches up to 2 days. Freeze for up to 1 month — reheat in oven or microwave.

## Leftover Ideas
- Use leftover croissants for bread pudding
- Make ham and cheese croissant sandwiches for lunch
