# Chicken Quesadillas

> The easiest possible dinner with a Costco rotisserie chicken. Crispy tortillas, melty cheese, shredded chicken, done.

**Prep:** 5 min | **Cook:** 10 min | **Servings:** 6 | **Cost:** ~$1.75/serving

## Costco Shopping List
- Kirkland Signature Rotisserie Chicken — 1 chicken (about 3 cups shredded) (~$4.99, 3 lb whole chicken)
- Kirkland Signature Shredded Mexican Blend Cheese — 2 cups (~$12.99, 2.5 lb bag)
- Kirkland Signature Flour Tortillas — 6 tortillas (~$4.49, 24 count)
- Kirkland Signature Organic Salsa — for serving (~$7.99, 2-pack 38 oz jars)

## Other Ingredients
- 1 tablespoon Butter or cooking spray
- 1/2 cup Sour cream *(optional, for serving)*

## Instructions
1. Shred the rotisserie chicken, discarding skin and bones. You need about 3 cups total.
2. Heat a large skillet or griddle over medium heat. Add a small pat of butter or a spray of cooking spray.
3. Lay a flour tortilla flat in the skillet. Spread about 1/3 cup of shredded cheese on one half, then top with about 1/2 cup of shredded chicken. Add another 1/3 cup of cheese on top of the chicken.
4. Fold the tortilla in half and press gently with a spatula.
5. Cook 2-3 minutes per side until the tortilla is golden brown and the cheese is fully melted.
6. Slide onto a cutting board, let rest 30 seconds, then cut into thirds.
7. Repeat with remaining tortillas. Serve with salsa and sour cream.

> **Tip:** The double cheese layer (under and over the chicken) is the move — it acts like glue and keeps the filling from falling out when you cut and eat.

## Storage
Refrigerate leftover quesadillas up to 3 days. Reheat in a dry skillet over medium heat to keep the tortilla crispy — the microwave will make them soggy.

## Leftover Ideas
- Chop leftover quesadillas into strips and toss on a salad
- Dip cold quesadilla wedges into guacamole for a quick snack
- Use remaining shredded chicken for chicken salad or soup
