# Slow Cooker Chicken Tortilla Soup

> Dump-and-go soup using Costco rotisserie chicken and pantry staples. Set it in the morning, eat it for dinner.

**Prep:** 15 min | **Cook:** 240 min | **Servings:** 8 | **Cost:** ~$2.00/serving

## Costco Shopping List
- Kirkland Signature Rotisserie Chicken — 1 chicken (about 4 cups shredded) (~$4.99, 3 lb whole chicken)
- Kirkland Signature Organic Chicken Broth — 2 cartons (64 oz total) (~$11.99, 6 x 32 oz cartons)
- Kirkland Signature Organic Diced Tomatoes — 2 cans (14.5 oz each) (~$7.99, 8 x 14.5 oz cans)
- Kirkland Signature Shredded Mexican Blend Cheese — 1 cup for topping (~$12.99, 2.5 lb bag)

## Other Ingredients
- 1 can (15 oz), drained and rinsed Black beans
- 1 cup (frozen or canned) Corn kernels
- 1 medium, diced Onion
- 3 cloves, minced Garlic
- 1 tablespoon Cumin
- 1 tablespoon Chili powder
- 2 limes, juiced Lime
- 1 handful per bowl, for serving Tortilla chips
- 1 sliced, for topping Avocado *(optional)*
- 1 dollop per bowl Sour cream *(optional)*

## Instructions
1. Shred the rotisserie chicken, discarding skin and bones.
2. Add chicken, broth, diced tomatoes, black beans, corn, onion, garlic, cumin, and chili powder to the slow cooker.
3. Cook on low for 6-8 hours or high for 3-4 hours.
4. Stir in lime juice just before serving.
5. Ladle into bowls. Top with crushed tortilla chips, shredded cheese, avocado, and sour cream.

## Storage
Refrigerate up to 5 days. Freezes beautifully for up to 3 months — freeze without toppings.

## Leftover Ideas
- Reduce the soup and use as a burrito filling
- Pour over rice for a soup-and-rice bowl
