# Costco Street Taco Bar for a Crowd

> Turn Costco's deli street taco kit into a full taco bar spread. Add your own toppings and you've got a party for 10 with almost zero effort.

**Prep:** 15 min | **Cook:** 10 min | **Servings:** 10 | **Cost:** ~$4.00/serving

## Costco Shopping List
- Chicken Street Tacos Kit — 2 kits (~$15.99, Serves 8-10 (seasoned chicken, tortillas, salsa, cilantro))
- Kirkland Signature Shredded Mexican Blend Cheese — 1 cup (~$12.99, 2.5 lb bag)
- Hass Avocados — 3 avocados (~$6.99, 5-pack)

## Other Ingredients
- 1 cup Sour cream
- 4 limes, quartered Limes
- 1 jar Pickled jalapenos
- 1 bottle (your favorite) Hot sauce
- 1/2 cup, crumbled Cotija cheese *(optional)*
- 4 thinly sliced Radishes *(optional)*

## Instructions
1. Heat the street taco kits according to package directions. Usually microwave or oven for 8-10 minutes.
2. While the chicken heats, prep your toppings: dice avocados, quarter limes, slice radishes, set out sour cream and cheese in small bowls.
3. Warm the tortillas from the kits in a dry skillet or wrapped in damp paper towels in the microwave for 30 seconds.
4. Set everything out buffet-style: chicken in the center, tortillas in a basket, toppings in individual bowls.

> **Tip:** Use a muffin tin to hold small toppings like jalapenos, sour cream, and lime wedges — looks great and keeps things organized.

5. Let everyone build their own tacos.

## Storage
Refrigerate leftover chicken up to 3 days. Use in quesadillas, salads, or rice bowls.

## Leftover Ideas
- Toss leftover chicken into scrambled eggs for breakfast tacos
- Mix with rice and beans for a burrito bowl
