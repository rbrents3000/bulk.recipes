# International Copycats

Costco-bulk versions of dishes from around the world — made with the same staples you're already buying.

## Ingredient Availability

Every recipe here is built around standard US Costco items: rotisserie chicken, Kirkland olive oil, bulk rice, frozen shrimp, etc. You won't need a special Costco for the Costco ingredients.

**However**, most international recipes call for specialty sauces, spices, or pastes under "Other Ingredients" that Costco doesn't carry. For those, your best bet is a local Asian, Latin, or international grocery store — they'll have better selection and better prices than a regular supermarket anyway. See [Your First Costco Trip](../../../guides/first-trip.md) for more on what to buy where.
