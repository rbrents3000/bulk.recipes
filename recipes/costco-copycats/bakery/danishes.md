# Copycat Costco Bakery Danishes

> Costco's bakery danishes come in cheese, apple, and cherry — all flaky, all sweet, all gone by noon. This recipe does all three fillings with a puff pastry shortcut that makes them shockingly easy.

**Prep:** 20 min | **Cook:** 18 min | **Servings:** 8 | **Cost:** ~$0.75/each

## Costco Shopping List
- Kirkland Signature Large Eggs — 1 egg for wash + 1 for filling (~$7.99, 5 dozen)
- Kirkland Signature Unsalted Butter — 2 tablespoons (~$12.99, 4 x 1 lb bars)

## Other Ingredients
**Base:**
- 1 package (17 oz) frozen puff pastry, thawed
- 1 egg beaten with 1 tablespoon water (egg wash)

**Cheese filling (for 8):**
- 4 oz cream cheese, softened
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1 egg yolk
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

**Apple filling (for 8):**
- 2 medium apples, peeled and diced small
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons brown sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon

**Cherry filling (for 8):**
- 1 cup canned cherry pie filling

**Glaze:**
- 1/2 cup powdered sugar
- 1 tablespoon milk
- 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. **Choose your filling:**
   - *Cheese:* Beat cream cheese, sugar, egg yolk, and vanilla until smooth.
   - *Apple:* Sauté diced apples in butter with brown sugar and cinnamon for 5 minutes until soft. Let cool.
   - *Cherry:* Just open the can. You're done.
3. Cut each puff pastry sheet into 4 squares (8 total). Place on baking sheet.
4. Score a 1/2-inch border around each square with a knife (don't cut all the way through). Prick the center with a fork.
5. Spoon 2 tablespoons of filling into the center of each square.
6. Brush the borders with egg wash.
7. Bake 16-18 minutes until puffed and deeply golden.
8. Mix glaze ingredients and drizzle over warm danishes.

> **Tip:** Mix and match the fillings — do 4 cheese and 4 apple, or make all three. One batch of puff pastry, three flavors.

## Storage
Best eaten the day they're made. Store at room temperature up to 2 days. Reheat in a 350°F oven for 5 minutes to re-crisp. You can freeze baked danishes for up to 1 month — wrap them individually and reheat from frozen at 350°F for about 10 minutes.

## Leftover Ideas
- Warm a cheese danish and serve with fresh berries for breakfast
- Crumble and use as a topping for ice cream
