# Copycat Costco Bakery Cinnamon Rolls

> Costco's bakery cinnamon rolls are enormous, gooey, and $7.99 for a tray of 12. These are just as big, just as soft, and your house will smell like a bakery for hours.

**Prep:** 30 min + 1.5 hr rise | **Cook:** 25 min | **Servings:** 12 rolls | **Cost:** ~$0.75/roll

## Costco Shopping List
- Kirkland Signature Unsalted Butter — 1/2 cup + 4 tablespoons (~$12.99, 4 x 1 lb bars)
- Kirkland Signature Large Eggs — 2 eggs (~$7.99, 5 dozen)

## Other Ingredients
**Dough:**
- 1 cup whole milk, warmed to 110°F
- 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast (1 packet)
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 4 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 cup melted butter
- 2 eggs

**Filling:**
- 1 cup packed brown sugar
- 2 1/2 tablespoons cinnamon
- 4 tablespoons softened butter

**Cream cheese frosting:**
- 4 oz cream cheese, softened
- 2 tablespoons butter, softened
- 1 cup powdered sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 tablespoon milk

## Instructions
1. Combine warm milk, yeast, and a pinch of sugar in a bowl. Let sit 5-10 minutes until foamy.
2. Mix in remaining sugar, melted butter, eggs, and salt. Add flour one cup at a time, stirring until a soft dough forms.
3. Knead on a floured surface for 5 minutes until smooth and elastic (or use a stand mixer with a dough hook for 5 minutes).
4. Place in a greased bowl, cover with a towel, and let rise 1 hour until doubled.
5. Roll dough into a large rectangle, about 16x20 inches. Spread softened butter across the surface, then sprinkle evenly with brown sugar and cinnamon.
6. Roll up tightly from the long side. Slice into 12 equal pieces. Place cut-side down in a greased 9x13 baking dish.
7. Cover and let rise 30 minutes until puffy.
8. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Bake 22-25 minutes until golden brown.
9. While baking, beat cream cheese, butter, powdered sugar, vanilla, and milk until smooth.
10. Spread frosting over warm rolls immediately.

> **Tip:** For make-ahead rolls, cover the pan after step 6 and refrigerate overnight. Pull out in the morning, let sit 30 minutes at room temperature, then bake.

## Storage
Cover and store at room temperature up to 2 days. Freeze baked rolls (unfrosted) for up to 2 months — reheat and frost fresh.

## Leftover Ideas
- Cube a stale roll and use it for bread pudding
- Slice in half and make a cinnamon roll French toast sandwich
