# Copycat Costco Chicken Street Tacos

> The deli's street taco kit is good. Yours will be better — juicy seasoned chicken, charred tortillas, and toppings that didn't come in a sealed compartment.

**Prep:** 15 min | **Cook:** 15 min | **Servings:** 8 (16 tacos) | **Cost:** ~$2.50/serving

## Costco Shopping List
- Kirkland Signature Boneless Skinless Chicken Thighs — 2 lbs (~$8.00, 6.5 lb pack)
- Kirkland Signature Flour Tortillas — 16 small/street size (~$4.49, 24 count)
- Hass Avocados — 2 (~$6.99, 5-pack)
- Kirkland Signature Organic Salsa Verde — 1/2 cup (~$7.99, 2-pack/38 oz)

## Other Ingredients
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 tablespoon chili powder
- 1 teaspoon cumin
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
- 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
- Juice of 2 limes
- 1/2 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
- 1 small white onion, finely diced
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Slice chicken thighs into thin strips. Toss with chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, smoked paprika, onion powder, salt, and half the lime juice.
2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet or on a griddle over high heat. Cook chicken strips 3-4 minutes per side until charred and cooked through. Let rest 2 minutes, then chop into small pieces.
3. Warm tortillas directly over a gas flame or in a dry skillet until slightly charred and pliable, about 30 seconds per side.
4. Dice avocados and toss with remaining lime juice and a pinch of salt.
5. Build tacos: tortilla, chicken, diced onion, avocado, cilantro, and a drizzle of salsa verde.

> **Tip:** Use thighs, not breasts. Thighs stay juicy at high heat and have more flavor — that's what the deli uses too.

## Storage
Refrigerate cooked chicken up to 4 days. Reheat in a hot skillet to re-crisp. Store toppings separately.

## Leftover Ideas
- Toss chicken into a quesadilla with leftover cheese
- Mix with rice and beans for a burrito bowl
- Scramble with eggs for breakfast tacos
