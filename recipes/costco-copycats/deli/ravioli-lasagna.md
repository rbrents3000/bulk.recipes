# Copycat Costco Ravioli Lasagna

> Layers of cheese ravioli, meat sauce, and melted cheese — Costco's deli shortcut lasagna that looks like you spent hours but actually takes 15 minutes to assemble.

**Prep:** 15 min | **Cook:** 45 min | **Servings:** 8 | **Cost:** ~$2.50/serving

## Costco Shopping List
- Kirkland Signature Cheese Ravioli — 2 lbs (~$10.99, 2-pack frozen)
- Kirkland Signature Ground Beef 88/12 — 1.5 lbs (~$6.99/lb, 4-pack patties or bulk tube)
- Kirkland Signature Marinara Sauce — 1 jar, 24 oz (~$8.99, 3-pack)
- Kirkland Signature Shredded Mozzarella — 2 cups (~$12.99, 2.5 lb bag)
- Kirkland Signature Shredded Parmigiano Reggiano — 1/2 cup (~$14.99, 1 lb tub)

## Other Ingredients
- 1 cup ricotta cheese
- 1/2 medium onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh basil for garnish *(optional)*

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. Brown ground beef in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add onion and cook 3-4 minutes. Add garlic and Italian seasoning, cook 1 minute. Stir in marinara sauce. Season with salt and pepper.
3. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 baking dish.
4. Layer: frozen ravioli in a single layer, half the remaining meat sauce, dollops of ricotta, 1/2 cup mozzarella. Repeat layers. Top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan.
5. Cover tightly with foil and bake 35 minutes. Remove foil and bake 10 more minutes until cheese is golden and bubbly.
6. Let rest 10 minutes before serving. Top with fresh basil.

> **Tip:** Use the ravioli frozen — they cook perfectly in the oven and absorb the sauce without getting mushy.

## Storage
Refrigerate up to 4 days. Reheat individual portions in the microwave or the whole dish at 350°F for 20 minutes. Freezes well for up to 3 months.

## Leftover Ideas
- Cut a square and pan-fry it in butter until crispy on both sides
- Scoop into a bowl, add broth, and call it ravioli soup
