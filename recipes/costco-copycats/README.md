# Costco Copycats

> Homemade versions of what Costco makes in-house — food court, deli, bakery, and international locations.

68 copycat recipes organized by where the original comes from. Every recipe has a Costco shopping list, step-by-step instructions, and a cost-per-serving breakdown that usually beats the original.

---

## [Food Court](food-court/) — 11 recipes

The $1.50 hot dog, the chicken bake, the churros, the pizza — recreated at home where the line is shorter and the portions are up to you.

## [Deli](deli/) — 32 recipes

Chicken marsala, salmon milano, shepherd's pie, yakisoba stir fry — the deli case, decoded. These are the prepared meals Costco's kitchen makes daily, now in your kitchen.

## [Bakery](bakery/) — 13 recipes

Pumpkin pie, cinnamon rolls, sheet cake, blueberry muffins — baked at home, Costco-sized. The pumpkin pie alone is worth the price of this repo (which is free, but still).

## [International](international/) — 12 recipes

Korean fried chicken, gyudon, char siu, tteokbokki — items from Costco locations around the world, made with ingredients you can find at a US Costco.
