# Copycat Costco Chicken Bake

> A homemade version of the iconic Costco food court chicken bake — chicken, bacon, cheese, and Caesar dressing wrapped in pizza dough.

**Prep:** 20 min | **Cook:** 25 min | **Servings:** 4 | **Cost:** ~$3.00/serving

## Costco Shopping List
- Kirkland Signature Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts — 2 breasts (about 1.5 lbs) (~$22.99, 6.5 lb pack)
- Kirkland Signature Thick-Sliced Bacon — 6 slices (~$18.99, 4 x 1 lb packs)
- Kirkland Signature Shredded Mexican Blend Cheese — 1.5 cups (or use mozzarella) (~$12.99, 2.5 lb bag)
- Kirkland Signature Shredded Parmigiano Reggiano — 1/2 cup grated (~$14.99, 1 lb tub)

## Other Ingredients
- 1 lb (store-bought or homemade) Pizza dough
- 1/2 cup Caesar dressing
- 1 teaspoon Garlic powder
- 1 beaten, for egg wash Egg
- 1 teaspoon Dried oregano *(optional)*

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. Season chicken breasts with garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Bake or pan-sear until cooked through (165°F internal). Let cool slightly, then dice into small cubes.
3. Cook bacon until crispy. Crumble or chop into small pieces.
4. In a bowl, combine diced chicken, bacon, mozzarella/Mexican cheese, Parmesan, and Caesar dressing. Mix well.
5. Divide pizza dough into 4 equal pieces. Roll each piece into a rectangle about 6x10 inches.
6. Place 1/4 of the filling down the center of each dough rectangle. Fold the sides over the filling and pinch to seal. Place seam-side down on the baking sheet.
7. Brush each chicken bake with beaten egg. Sprinkle with a little extra Parmesan and oregano if desired.
8. Bake for 20-25 minutes until golden brown.

> **Tip:** Let them cool for 5 minutes before cutting — the filling is molten hot.

## Storage
Refrigerate up to 3 days. Reheat in oven at 350°F for 10-15 minutes. Can freeze unbaked for up to 2 months.

## Leftover Ideas
- Slice and serve over salad for a deconstructed chicken bake bowl
- Dice up leftovers and fold into scrambled eggs for a loaded breakfast
