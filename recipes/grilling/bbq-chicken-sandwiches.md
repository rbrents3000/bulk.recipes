# BBQ Chicken Sandwiches

> Rotisserie chicken's second act — shredded, sauced, and stacked on a brioche bun with crunchy slaw.

**Prep:** 15 min | **Cook:** 5 min | **Servings:** 6 | **Cost:** ~$2.50/serving

## Costco Shopping List
- Kirkland Signature Rotisserie Chicken — 1 whole chicken, shredded (~$4.99, whole chicken)
- Kirkland Signature BBQ Sauce — 1 cup (~$8.99, 2-pack bottles)
- Kirkland Signature Brioche Buns — 6 buns (~$5.49, 12 count)

## Other Ingredients
- 2 cups Green cabbage, thinly shredded
- 1 medium Carrot, grated
- 2 tablespoons Apple cider vinegar
- 1 tablespoon Mayonnaise
- 1 teaspoon Sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon Salt
- to taste Hot sauce *(optional)*

## Instructions
1. Shred the rotisserie chicken, discarding skin and bones. You should get about 3–4 cups of meat.
2. Toss shredded chicken with BBQ sauce in a saucepan over medium heat. Stir until warmed through, about 5 minutes.
3. While the chicken heats, make the slaw: toss cabbage and carrot with vinegar, mayo, sugar, and salt. It should be tangy and crunchy — this is what keeps the sandwich from being one-note.
4. Toast the brioche buns lightly — cut-side down in a dry skillet or under the broiler for 30 seconds.
5. Pile BBQ chicken on the bottom bun, top with a generous mound of slaw, and close it up.

> **Tip:** The slaw gets better after sitting 10 minutes, so make it first and let it hang out while you prep everything else.

## Storage
Store shredded BBQ chicken and slaw separately in the fridge up to 3 days. Assemble sandwiches fresh — soggy buns are nobody's friend.

## Leftover Ideas
- Pile the BBQ chicken onto nachos with cheese and jalapenos
- Stuff into a quesadilla with shredded cheese
- Serve the chicken over a baked potato with sour cream
