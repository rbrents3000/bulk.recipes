# Meat & Poultry

> Where buying a whole cow starts to feel like a reasonable life decision.

Costco's meat department is the real reason you drove past three other grocery stores. The quality-to-price ratio here is genuinely hard to beat — we're talking USDA Choice beef, Kirkland Signature chicken that actually tastes like chicken, and pork shoulders big enough to feed a block party. Yes, you'll need freezer space. No, that's not a problem — it's a lifestyle.

## Chicken

| Product | Typical Price | Package Size | Why It's Worth It |
|---------|--------------|-------------|-------------------|
| Kirkland Signature Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts | ~$3.49/lb | 6-pack, ~$25 | Individually sealed — grab one, freeze the rest |
| Kirkland Boneless Skinless Chicken Thighs | ~$2.99/lb | ~6 lbs | More flavor than breasts, more forgiving to cook |
| Whole Young Chicken (raw) | ~$1.49/lb | Single bird | The cheapest protein in the store, and incredibly versatile |
