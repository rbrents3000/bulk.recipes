# Frozen

> The freezer aisle: where meal prep dreams go to thrive and produce guilt goes to die.

Here's the thing about Costco's frozen section — it's not where you go when you've given up on cooking. It's where you go when you've gotten smart about it. Flash-frozen fruits and vegetables are picked at peak ripeness and locked in at their nutritional best, which means that bag of frozen broccoli is arguably *fresher* than the sad bunch that's been sitting in your crisper drawer for nine days. Plus, the prices per pound make fresh produce blush. Keep your freezer stocked and you'll always be ten minutes away from something good.
