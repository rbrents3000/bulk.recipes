# China Food Court Menu

Costco opened its first mainland China warehouse in Shanghai in 2019 and it was a phenomenon — the store had to close early on opening day due to overcrowding. The food court caters to Chinese tastes while keeping the Costco classics.

**Locations:** 7 warehouses (Shanghai, Suzhou, Hangzhou, Shenzhen, Nanjing, Ningbo, Guangzhou)
