# Pantry Staples

> The aisle where you walk in for olive oil and walk out with a 25-pound bag of flour you didn't know you needed.

This is the backbone of your kitchen. Costco's pantry section is where bulk buying actually makes the most sense — these things don't spoil quickly, you use them constantly, and the per-unit savings are genuinely ridiculous. A bag of Kirkland jasmine rice costs less than two takeout containers of fried rice, and it'll make about forty of them. Stock up, stack your shelves, and never panic about "what's for dinner" again.
