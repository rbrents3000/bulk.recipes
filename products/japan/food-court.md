# Japan Food Court Menu

Costco Japan has one of the most interesting food courts in the world. The core US items exist alongside Japanese-exclusive offerings that are genuinely great.

**Locations:** 33 warehouses across Japan (Tokyo, Osaka, Sapporo, Fukuoka, etc.)

## Seasonal Specials

Japan rotates seasonal items frequently:
- **Summer:** Mango soft serve, cold noodle salads
- **Winter:** Clam chowder bread bowls, holiday pizza varieties
- **Spring:** Sakura-themed desserts occasionally
