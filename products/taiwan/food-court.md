# Taiwan Food Court Menu

Costco Taiwan has a vibrant food court with strong local flavors. Taiwan's night market culture means the bar is high — and the food court delivers with items like bubble tea, fried chicken buckets, and beef noodle soup.

**Locations:** 14 warehouses across Taiwan (Taipei, Taichung, Kaohsiung, etc.)
