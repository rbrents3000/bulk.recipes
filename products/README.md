## Fun Facts

- **Hot dogs:** Pork-based in Japan, Taiwan, China, and Australia; all-beef in the US
- **Chicken bake replacements:** Bulgogi bake (Japan, Taiwan), pork bake (Korea), seafood bake (Taiwan), Mexican chicken bake (Sweden)
- **Gelato** replaces soft serve in Iceland, Sweden, and sometimes UK
- **Bubble tea** available in Korea, Taiwan, China, and Australia
- **Pizza** is customized everywhere: bulgogi (Korea), truffle/Peking duck (China), seafood (Japan/Taiwan), al pastor (Mexico)
- The **$1.50 hot dog combo** maintains roughly the same price point worldwide
