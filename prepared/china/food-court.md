# China Food Court Menu

Costco opened its first mainland China warehouse in Shanghai in 2019 and it was a phenomenon — the store had to close early on opening day due to overcrowding. The food court is wildly creative, with unique pizza toppings, multiple soft serve flavors, and regional items.

**Locations:** 7 warehouses (Shanghai, Suzhou, Hangzhou, Shenzhen, Nanjing, Ningbo, Guangzhou)

## Seasonal

- **Mid-Autumn Festival:** Pork mooncakes
- **Rotating:** Soft serve flavors change frequently
- **Regional:** Some items only at specific city locations (e.g., okonomiyaki at Suzhou)

## Notes

- Costco China has been wildly popular — membership cards sold out before stores opened
- The food court often sells out of popular items by mid-afternoon
- Soft serve flavors are far more adventurous than any other country
- Pork hot dogs (not beef) — same as Japan and Taiwan
