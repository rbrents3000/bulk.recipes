# Spain Deli & Prepared Foods

Costco Spain's deli is where the bulk-buying impulse meets the tapas instinct. The deli case leans heavily into Spanish classics — **jamón, croquetas, tortilla, paella kits** — alongside the usual Costco standards.

If you've ever wished you could serve an entire tapas spread without actually cooking, this is your section.

### Summer Seasonal (May–September)
- **Gazpacho** and **salmorejo** in family-size containers — the deli case practically turns into a cold soup bar
- **Ensaladilla rusa** — Spanish-style potato salad with tuna, peas, and mayo. A summer tapa essential.
- Lighter paella kits emphasizing seafood over meat

### Christmas Seasonal (November–January)
- **Turrón** — Spanish nougat in two styles: Alicante (hard, whole almonds) and Jijona (soft, fudge-like). Sold in bulk slabs.
- **Polvorones** — Crumbly almond shortbread from Andalucía. They disintegrate on contact — that's the point.
- **Mantecados** — Similar to polvorones but denser, in cinnamon, lemon, and chocolate.
- **Roscón de Reyes ingredients** — Marzipan, candied fruit, and cream for DIY versions (see bakery for the full roscón).
