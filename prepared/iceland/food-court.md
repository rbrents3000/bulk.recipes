# Iceland Food Court Menu

Yes, there's a Costco in Iceland. One warehouse in Garðabær, just south of Reykjavik. The food court trades soft serve for gelato — very Scandinavian.

**Locations:** 1 warehouse (Garðabær, near Reykjavik)

## Notes

- Iceland has one of the highest costs of living in the world, so even Costco prices are higher
- The gelato replaces soft serve — same as Sweden and UK
- Icelandic hot dogs (pylsur) are traditionally made with a mix of lamb, pork, and beef
- This is the smallest Costco market in the world by warehouse count
