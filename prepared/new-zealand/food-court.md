# New Zealand Food Court Menu

Costco New Zealand opened its first (and so far only) warehouse in Auckland. The food court shares a lot with Australia but has a few Kiwi touches.

**Locations:** 1 warehouse (Westgate, Auckland — opened 2022)

## New Zealand Items

| Item | Price (NZD) | Price (~USD) | Description |
|------|------------|-------------|-------------|
| Southern Style Chicken Sandwich | $8.99 | ~$5.40 | Battered chicken, lettuce, tomato, cheese, sauce — shared with Australia |
| Chicken Tenders & Chips | $8.99 | ~$5.40 | Not available in the US food court |
| Meat Pie | $4.99 | ~$3.00 | Classic NZ/Aussie meat pie — golden pastry with beef filling |
