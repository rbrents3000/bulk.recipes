# New Zealand Deli & Prepared Foods

New Zealand has exactly one Costco — the **Westgate warehouse in Auckland**, opened in 2022. Opening day traffic jams made national news, which in a country of five million people is really saying something.

The deli case leans into local favorites alongside the usual Costco staples, and the **lamb situation** alone is worth the membership.

### Summer/Christmas Seasonal (December–February)

NZ summer and Christmas overlap — outdoor eating, BBQ prep, and holiday entertaining all at once:

- **Pavlova** gets extra prominence — it *is* Christmas in New Zealand. Party-sized, pre-topped with cream and fruit.
- **Lamb cutlets and marinated meats** get extra shelf space — straight from the deli case to the barbie.
- **Kumara salad** becomes a BBQ side staple — travels well to the beach, the bach, or the backyard.
- **Prawn platters** and seafood items increase — summer = seafood season.

### Winter Seasonal (June–August)

NZ winters are mild but wet and grey enough to demand comfort food:

- **Family Meat Pie** gets top billing — the ultimate cold-weather meal
- **Sausage rolls** sell faster — hot pastry + cold rain = perfect match
- **Lamb stew kits** take center stage — NZ lamb at its best, pre-marinated for easy weeknight dinners
- **Soups and stews** appear in the prepared foods section

> **Fun fact:** The Westgate Costco reportedly signed up more members before opening day than any other single-warehouse market in Costco history. Auckland was ready. Some members drove from as far as Hamilton (a 90-minute trip) just to shop on opening weekend, which tells you everything about how Kiwis felt about finally getting their own Costco.
