# New Zealand Deli & Prepared Foods

New Zealand has exactly one Costco — the Westgate warehouse in Auckland, opened in 2022. On opening day, the traffic jams were so spectacular they made national news, which in a country of five million people is really saying something. Kiwis had been watching Australians haul home bulk toilet paper for years, and when their turn finally came, they showed up in force. The deli case here leans into local favorites alongside the usual Costco staples, and the lamb situation alone is worth the membership.
