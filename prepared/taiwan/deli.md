# Taiwan Deli & Prepared Foods

Costco Taiwan's deli mixes US standards with Taiwanese and East Asian prepared foods. The sushi and bento items are particularly strong — this is a country where convenience store food is already excellent, so Costco has to bring its A-game. It does. The lu rou fan kit alone is worth the membership.

## Seasonal Items

- **Dragon Boat Festival (June):** Zongzi (sticky rice dumplings wrapped in bamboo leaves) — pork, mushroom, chestnut, and egg yolk fillings
- **Mid-Autumn Festival (Sept-Oct):** Mooncake gift boxes and BBQ meat platters (Taiwanese tradition is to grill outdoors during this holiday)
- **Chinese New Year (Jan-Feb):** Nian cai (New Year feast dishes) — braised pork knuckle, whole fish, and cold appetizer platters
