### January {#january}

The slow cooker is the MVP of your kitchen. Everything is cold, dark, and begging to be braised.

**Events:** New Year's, comfort food season — nobody wants a salad right now
**In the bakery:** King Cake (Mardi Gras preview), holiday cookie clearance
**In the deli:** Pot pies, stews, braised meats — winter comfort food at its peak
**International:** Mexico: Rosca de Reyes (Three Kings Cake). Korea: Lunar New Year prep. Japan: osechi items.
**Southern Hemisphere:** Australia/NZ: Peak summer — BBQ, fresh berries, pavlova, Christmas leftovers still in the fridge
