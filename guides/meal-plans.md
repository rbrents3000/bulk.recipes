## Tips for All Tiers

- **Shop on Wednesday or Thursday** — Costco restocks midweek, and weekends are a contact sport.
- **Buy the big bag of rice once** — It'll last through multiple weeks of meal plans.
- **The rotisserie chicken is your secret weapon** — At $4.99, it's the best deal in the store. They reportedly sell them at a loss. Take advantage.
- **Check the manager's markdown section** — Meat nearing its sell-by date is perfect for cooking that night or freezing immediately.
- **Bring a cooler bag** — Especially in summer. That shrimp doesn't want to sit in a hot car for 45 minutes while you debate throw pillows.
